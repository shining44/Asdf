Tomoca's legendary Harar coffee has been the cornerstone of our legacy since 1953. Sourced from the ancient coffee forests of Eastern Ethiopia, these 100% Arabica beans are hand-selected and expertly roasted using traditional Italian techniques. The result is a remarkably smooth, full-bodied coffee with deep chocolate notes and a lingering, satisfying finish. This is the same coffee that has made Tomoca famous throughout Addis Ababa and beyond.
